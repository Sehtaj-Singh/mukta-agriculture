About Mukta Agriculture

Mukta Agriculture is committed to empowering farmers with reliable, high-quality agricultural equipment that improves productivity and supports sustainable farming practices.

With years of experience in the agricultural tools and machinery industry, we specialize in supplying rotavators, cultivators, seeders, and tractor attachments tailored to the real needs of farmers. Our focus is on durability, performance, and long-term value.

We believe that strong farming starts with the right equipment. That’s why Mukta Agriculture partners closely with farmers to understand field conditions and deliver solutions that work reliably season after season.
